Medical Billing Practices

Our services encompass patient registration, insurance verification, charge capture, claims submission, and follow-up. We employ skilled professionals to accurately code medical services, ensuring compliance with industry standards and maximizing reimbursement. Our team is dedicated to resolving claim denials, managing patient accounts, and providing detailed financial reports to empower healthcare providers with actionable insights.

Revenue Cycle Management & Optimization

We offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to optimize healthcare revenue cycle. Our expertise lies in streamlining processes, ensuring accurate and timely claims submission, and maximizing revenue recovery. We specialize in patient registration, insurance verification, charge capture, coding, claims submission, and follow-up, providing healthcare providers with the tools and support necessary to improve financial performance and patient satisfaction.

New Practice Support

SmartCode can help emerging medical practice by establishing robust revenue cycle management systems. Our expertise lies in developing tailored strategies, from initial practice setup to comprehensive billing and coding processes. We guide new practices through insurance verification, charge capture, claims submission, and follow-up, ensuring maximum reimbursement and minimal claim denials. By focusing on operational efficiency and compliance, we empower new healthcare providers to concentrate on delivering quality patient care while we manage the financial aspects of their practice.

Accounts Receivable Follow up

Smartcode Medical Business Solutions excels in managing your accounts receivable, ensuring timely and accurate claim reimbursements. Our dedicated team employs robust follow-up strategies to expedite payment collection, minimize claim denials, and improve your cash flow. We utilize advanced technology and industry expertise to efficiently resolve outstanding claims, ultimately enhancing your financial health. Also we do auditing activities for contractual arrangement, compliancy checks for receivable accounts reconciliation and provide a regular reporting for revenue performance and status.
